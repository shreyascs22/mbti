FUNCTIONS

Si :- Types with this function tend to think and rely based on their past experiences to make decisions in their life. They prefer working on methods which have been tried and tested before.

Fi :- Types with this function weigh their life or internal self before expressing it and tend to keep these emotions internally itself. People with this type focus on assessing the world based on what they feel is authentic and right for individuals.

Ti :- Types with this function are highly logical in nature. They are analytical and very objective in nature.They turn inwards to come up with their own theories, opinions and solutions to the outside world.

Ni :- Types with this function are rational and can make patterns easily. They think about the future rather than the past and search for guidance internally.

Se :- Types with this function take in as much sensory information as possible this makes them very detail-oriented and observant of the world around them. This function allows people to be present and live in the moment.

Fe :- Types with this function interact with the world around. They make decisions based the outward environment and how their feelings will affect their physical world.

Te :- Types with this function are very goal oriented. They value things which would result in a useful output.

Ne :- Types with this function are creative and characterized by their ingenuity. They normally brainstorm their thoughts and come up with ideas.
